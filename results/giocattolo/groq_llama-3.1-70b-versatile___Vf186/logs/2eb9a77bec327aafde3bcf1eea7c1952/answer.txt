Yes, Pink Floyd was an influence on Radiohead, as the band members listed them among their earliest influences, alongside other notable artists such as Queen and Bob Dylan. Critics also compared Radiohead's work, particularly their album OK Computer, to Pink Floyd's 1973 album The Dark Side of the Moon. Additionally, Pink Floyd is mentioned as one of the bands that Radiohead succeeded as a "touchstone for everything that is fearless and adventurous in rock".
